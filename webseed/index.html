Welcome to IIIF in a Box!

Your virtual server has been set up with the following software:

Loris

Loris is a full-featured IIIF image server. It can efficiently serve images at various scales, saving bandwidth for larger images and allowing them to be easily viewed by IIIF viewers such as Mirador and Universal Viewer

Drag additional files into the loris folder to serve them through Loris. They will be served at this URL:

http://127.0.0.1:8181/loris/synced/<name of your file>

Omeka

Omeka is a content management system optimized for displaying library, museum, archival and scholarly collections. Build rich exhibits with its built-in exhibits builder and extend it with numerous free plugins and themes.

Click here to start using your Omeka instance. The default credentials are:

superuser / omekasuper

IIIF Toolkit

IIIF Toolkit is a plugin that facilitates various IIIF-related operations in Omeka. Import IIIF content from existing sources, link uploaded images to Loris, and annotate and examine your content with Mirador viewer.

This plugin has been enabled and pre-configured for you by the installer. Sign into Omeka to use.
